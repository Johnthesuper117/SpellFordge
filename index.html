HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Survive infinite waves of magical enemies. Defeat creatures to earn souls and collect healing orbs. After each wave, choose spell upgrades to grow stronger and unlock new abilities.

Combat

Move: WASD or Arrow Keys

Cast Spell: Click Mouse Anywhere

Switch Spell: Q to switch forward, E to switch backward

Learn all 12 spells and their unique effects. Chain attacks together for powerful combos.

Abilities (Secondary)

Press Spacebar to activate. 7 unique abilities: Shield, Blink, Slow, Heal, Pulse, Clone, Freeze.

Each has a cooldown. Choose new ones after every 3 waves.

Resources

Mana (Health): Your life pool. Collect green orbs to restore health.

Souls: Points earned from kills. Proves your mastery.

Status Effects

Burn (Red Aura): Enemy takes continuous damage over time.

Chill (Blue Outline): Enemy moves 50% slower.

Pro Tips

  • Combine Fire + Frost spells for maximum damage synergy
  • Dodge incoming enemies when possible to avoid taking damage
  • Prioritize damage upgrades early, defense upgrades later
  • Use secondary abilities strategically—don't waste them
  • Each spell has different pierce, explosion, or chain properties
