HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Survive infinite waves of magical enemies. Defeat creatures to earn souls and collect healing orbs. After each wave, choose spell upgrades to grow stronger and unlock new abilities.

Combat

Move: WASD or Arrow Keys

Cast Spell (Primary): Left-click to cast the currently selected spell.

Artifact (Secondary): Right-click or press your secondary key (default SPACE) to activate your equipped Artifact. Artifacts cost Mana to use and have cooldowns.

Switch Spell: Q to switch forward, E to switch backward. You can equip up to 5 primary Spells. When you learn a 6th, you'll be prompted to replace one.

Each Spell has unique effects and trait tags. Build synergies by collecting multiple items with the same trait.

Abilities (Secondary)

Press Spacebar to activate. 7 unique abilities: Shield, Blink, Slow, Heal, Pulse, Clone, Freeze.

Each has a cooldown. Choose new ones after every 3 waves.

Resources

Health: Your life pool. If this reaches 0 you die and return to the menu.

Mana: Used by Artifacts and some Spells. Mana regenerates over time; Artifacts may increase regen or provide passive bonuses.

Souls: Points earned from kills. Use them conceptually for scoring and flavor.

Status Effects

Burn (Red Aura): Enemy takes continuous damage over time.

Chill (Blue Outline): Enemy moves 50% slower.

Pro Tips

  • Combine spell traits (synergies) and Artifacts that share traits to unlock bonuses (e.g. Arcane).
  • Dodge incoming enemies — positioning matters more than raw damage sometimes.
  • Upgrades are offered between waves — choose wisely: new spells, artifacts, or upgrades.
  • Secondaries (Artifacts) are powerful but cost Mana; manage your resource carefully.
  • Mini-bosses appear every 5 waves and bosses every 10 waves — be prepared.
